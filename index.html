About me

Hi! I’m Dr. Yolanda Gonzalez PsyD, LMHC. Today you are one step closer to a new you – a you, who will feel empowered and embark on a positive journey towards growth and well-being.

With a Doctoral of Psychology degree, Dual Masters in Mental Health & Marital, Couple & Family Therapy/Counseling as well as my Masters in Clinical Psychology, I have been trained in various efficacious evidence based treatments to effectively help you cope with your daily life stressors.

I have founded this mental health practice in hopes of fostering a safe and peaceful environment for moms to come and fuel their hearts with hope, comfort and love.

Our services

We offer a friendly and down-to-earth approach to therapy, providing a safe, confidential and non-judgmental space to talk. We either come to you, or you can come to us. We can go to your home, or engage in “walk and talk” by either visiting your local park or walking around your neighborhood. Children are welcome to come while you are in therapy, just let us know in advance so we can provide toys for your child to play with. We also offer remote psychoeducational testing, provided there is a quiet room free of distractions (please call us for more information). We often test your child at their school, or local library. With training and experience in the main evidence-based psychotherapies, we work flexibly and collaboratively with other clinicians, tailoring therapy to the needs of each client we see.
